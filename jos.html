Joshua Ashford Year Up Student

My name Joshua Ashford and I was born in Alabama in 1996. I have moved around to various states during my childhood. Since then I have loved to travel and experience new things throughout my life. Traveling has opened my eyes and helped me be more of a dynamic person in the long run. Also, it has shaped the goals that I have for myself in the near and distant future. My first goal is to finish my internship and graduate Year Up, so I can get my college credits and come out with a certification. I can use all these achievements to update my resume and increase my chances of getting a job. My second goal is to go back to school and complete the rest of my years at Southern Illinois University. I want to come out with a computer science degree and get a job in that career field. Lastly, I eventually want to further my education by going to graduate school after I am working and am financially stable.
