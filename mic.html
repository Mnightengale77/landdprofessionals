Michelle Nightengale Year Up Student

My name is Michelle Nightengale and I was born and raised in Chicago. I am currently enrolled in Year Up – Chicago where I learn the skills to become an IT. These skills include computer hardware and software, web development, and programming. Year Up is a one-year accredited work-force development program that teaches IT and professional skills. I joined this program to help pave the way to my success by not only teaching me the skills I need for my career but because of the standards and environment this program has. The program holds everyone accountable for upholding the highest standard of the corporate world while still having an inviting and supportive community. This is important to me because I hold myself to the highest standard. I keep myself accountable, and I always know when there is room for improvement. I stay dependable, diligent, and open-minded. I enjoy collaborating with others and lending a hand. I also enjoy taking a leadership role because everyone trusts me to oversee them and make sure the task is done correctly. After this program, I aim to go to college. While in the program, I will decide what specific career I want, and the steps I need to take.
